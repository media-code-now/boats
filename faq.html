FAQ

FAQ

General information:
  • Open 7 days a week from 8 AM to 6 PM (unless adverse weather forces otherwise).
  • Rental fee will be charged in full at time of booking.
  • Security deposit will be held on the day of rental.
  • All operators of vehicles must be at least 21 years old.
  • All pricing is per vessel, not per person. All vessels have a towing trailer at no extra cost.
  • A sales tax of 6% will be applied to all fees.

    • Do I need a boating license?
  • In order to operate a motorboat of ten (10) horsepower or greater, Florida law requires anyone who was born on or after Jan. 1, 1988 to successfully complete an approved boating safety course and obtain a Boating Safety Education Identification Card issued by the FWC.

    • Where can I use the boat/jet ski?
  • Sunny Isles Beach / Bal Harbour

    • How long can I rent the boat/jet ski?
  • Our daily rental is from 8 AM to 6 PM.
  • Late drop off (6 AM, morning after rental) is available for an extra fee.
  • Any watercraft returned after 8 AM will incur an additional rental fee of one day.
  • Any rental not returned by the due time and date is subject to additional fees.

    • How do I book/reserve a rental?
  • For your convenience, we have an online booking system on this website.
  • Select the Book Rental icon, choose your rental period and watercraft. The system then checks the database for availability.
  • Once you finish booking and have a credit card on file, you will receive a confirmation email (check SPAM folder as well).
  • We offer assistance through the phone if you are experiencing any difficulties on line. Call us at (702) 744-7314.

    • Does the boat/jet ski need extra gas throughout the day?
  • You will receive your rental with a full tank of gas. If you do run out during the day, there are gas pumps available in Lake Mead.
  • You must return the rental with a full tank of gas.
  • If the vehicle does not have a full tank of gas upon return, you may be subject to an additional fee.
  • The typical consumption is 20-40 gallons a day.

    • Trailers:
  • Trailers are included with each rental at no extra cost.
  • If your vehicle is not equipped with a flat-4 light outlet, adapters and complete wire kits are available at most auto part stores or U-Haul.
  • All trailers require a 2” ball.
  • Trailers have “flat 4 pin” light connectors. “Round 7 pin” adapters are available if needed at no extra cost.
  • Bumper style hitches are sufficient for single and double jet ski trailers.
  • We offer to loan a slide and ball for customers with a tow package.

    • Cancellation Policy:
  • If you cancel over a week before your rental a 10% cancellation fee applies.
  • If your reservation needs to be rescheduled due to weather conditions, we will do so at no extra cost.
  • cancellations within 2 to 7 days... a 50% cancellation fee applies.
  • If you can not reschedule due to weather conditions, we will issue a refund with 10% cancellation fee.
  • Cancellations will not be accepted within 48 hours of your scheduled reservation pickup time.
